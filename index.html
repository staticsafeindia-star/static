What is Electrostatic Discharge (ESD)? expand_more
Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) is the sudden transfer of static electricity between two objects. Even a small static discharge can permanently damage sensitive electronic components during manufacturing or handling.
Why is ESD Protection Critical? expand_more
Proper ESD protection prevents component failures, improves product reliability, reduces manufacturing defects and helps maintain international quality standards.
Which Industries Require ESD Protection? expand_more
Electronics manufacturing, PCB assembly, semiconductor, automotive electronics, medical devices, laboratories, cleanrooms and aerospace industries rely on ESD-safe environments.
Do You Deliver Across India? expand_more
Yes. We supply ESD products to manufacturing facilities, industrial parks and production plants across India through our nationwide distribution network.

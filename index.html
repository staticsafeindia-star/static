Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about our ESD products and partnership process.

What is ESD?expand_more
ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) is the sudden transfer of static electricity between objects. It can damage sensitive electronic components and affect manufacturing processes.
Why is ESD protection important?expand_more
Proper ESD protection helps prevent product failures, improves quality, reduces rejection rates and protects sensitive electronic assemblies.
Which industries require ESD products?expand_more
Electronics manufacturing, PCB assembly, EMS companies, semiconductor facilities, medical device manufacturing, cleanrooms and automotive electronics industries commonly require ESD protection.
Do you supply products across India?expand_more
Yes. StaticSafe India Pvt. Ltd. supplies ESD and Anti-Static products across India.
Do you provide ESD protected area setup?expand_more
Yes, we provide comprehensive consultation and setup services for EPA (Electrostatic Protected Areas) to ensure full compliance with international standards.
